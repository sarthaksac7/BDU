Anuya Chauhan Kudecha

Anuya Chauhan Kudecha is a partner in Dancing Shiva Productions (producers of feature films and web series) and TheDigitalHash.com (a digital enetrtainemnt website)

She has worked with top advertising agencies and played a key role organising launch and post-launch brand campaigns across print, TV, radio and ambient (BTL) media. She formulated effective communication strategies to build brand recall across rural and urban markets.

"Before getting a break in the field I wanted to pursue, I worked with Harmony, a monthly magazine targeted at senior citizens and an initiative from the Anil Dhirubhai Ambani Group," she said.

‘’When I was not thinking of ways for branding and to boost magazine sales and advertising revenue, I was looking for a break in the film production and marketing companies. ‘’Eventually I got that break and joined Culture Brandz in their film marketing division. I worked on few films such as Azaan, Tukka Fit before the entrepreneurial bug bit my colleague Ritesh Kudecha in 2012 and together we started Hash Entertainment.’’

After marketing close to 20+ films within 5 years under Hash Entertainment, we started a Partnership firm – Dancing Shiva with an objective to

Make content-driven feature films and web series. Launch www.digitalhash.com: a digital entertainment website covering online news, reviews and features.

Anuya leads a team of 15 people and within a span of just 2 years the firm has produced 4 Web Series and successfully launched properties such as “Streaming Awards” and “Streaming Calendar” on its web platform www.digitalhash.com

Dancing Shiva has recently produced Still About Section 377 which is currently live on Sony Liv. Besides Dancing Shiva, Anuya is a co-producer in the upcoming thriller titled “The Tashkent Files” which is directed by Vivek Agnihotri and is running successfully at the box office Worldwide
