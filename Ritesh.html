Ritesh Kudecha

With an experience spread across 15+ years in the field of Entertainment and Films, Ritesh has worked with leading Media Companies such as Group M and established film studios such as Shemaroo and Reliance Entertainment.

Teaming up with his partner and wife, Anuya Chauhan, he formed Dancing Shiva to create content for global consumption. With the exponential growth of Digital Entertainment, content today crosses boundaries and finds itself on more screens than just cinemas and television. Video content is now consumed on the go, on mobile phones and tablets too. With all these exhibition formats and the importance of video content in local languages and global reach, we strive to deliver entertainment that audiences can consumed in the most convenient manner, be it cinema, smart TVs, mobile phones, tablets or computers.

The goal is to work with like-minded people and strike meaningful alliances with global partners, who share the same vision of the creation, growth and sustainability of the content and business.

Dancing Shiva is currently producing and co-producing 5 web series, a Hindi feature film and a Marathi feature film with established partners in their respective domains. 

Filmography for Ritesh Kudecha includes:
The Tashkent Files (2019) – Producer
Still About Section 377 (2018) – Producer
Friends in Law (2018) – Associate Producer
